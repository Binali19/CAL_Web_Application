Analyzing Financial Data using Machine Learning and Statistical Techniques

ABOUT US

Capital Alliance (Pvt) Limited (CAL) is one of Sri Lanka's leading investment institutions participating in both primary and equity markets, with a strategic interest in using financial data analytics to enhance financial market forecasts, thereby improving both quality and return on investment.

SLIIT has always been extremely keen on developing an enriching educational and research as one of Sri Lanka's leading higher education institutes. And also committed to encouraging and developing new technologies.

SLIIT and CAL are therefore working as willing partners with a common goal of establishing a research laboratory for Financial Data Analytics, allowing those interested to perform comprehensive and detailed research on educational and industrial relevance.

OUR OBJECTIVES

To explore the potential directions current and future research can take, and to build local expertise in the area of financial data analytics in order to make better investment decisions.

To share the expertise assimilated and generated throughout the process via reputable scientific journals with the academic community, effectively increasing the exposure of local research conducted within this field.
